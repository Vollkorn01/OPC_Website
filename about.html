About Us Obrobibini Peace Complex is a non-governmental organisation in Ghana that promotes social entrepreneurship in the field of regenerative food and farming systems. Our centres connect national and international stakeholders and enable them to co-develop applied solutions.

We are based in Busua, a colourful fishing village in Ghana

Busua is one of the country’s most famous beach resorts and focal point of Ghana’s nascent surfing scene.

Centre of Excellence for Sustainable Agroforestry

Our second centre near Mankessim (Central Region) covers more than 40 acres. We cultivate and process different organic crops (e.g. maize (corn), cassava, pineapple, vegetables, etc.), do research trials and plan the setting up of a locally adapted dynamic agroforestry system.

Our Vision

Our vision is to shape a new generation of leaders in the field of regenerative food and farming systems.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make regenerative living mainstream through professional education (vocational training and social entrepreneurship). Our holistic approach also entails circular economy (e.g. natural sanitation, proper waste management and renewable energy) and complementary medicine.
