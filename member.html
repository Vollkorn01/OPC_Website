Help us to run Obrobibini Peace Complex!

We are a charitable association based in Switzerland, and we need your help!

Membership

Two ways to help us

1. Apply for active membership (below) to help running the association

Benefits

  • Contribute to a meaningful cause
  • Regular updates about our activities (WhatsApp group)
  • Voting right at the annual member assembly with African food and drinks (if in-person meeting)

Duties

  • Dedicate some of your time using your own skills to help us grow
  • Minimum membership fee of 100 CHF (50 CHF for students, elderly/disabled, unemployed)

Positions you can apply for

  • Actuary (board member) – Keep minutes and archive, membership administration
  • Treasurer (board member) – Finances, accounting, book keeping (whole board bears responsibility)
  • Editor – Write for OPC
  • Designer – Design for OPC
  • Public Relations – Inspire more people for our cause (active/passive members)
  • Webmaster – Manage OPC website
  • Membership Ambassador – Care for and represent membership
  • Fund-Raiser – Organize fundraising campaigns (events, crowdfunding, etc.)
  • Materials Procurement – Organize gathering and storage of materials to be shipped to Ghana
  • Other – Other skills you bring to OPC not covered above

2. Sign up for passive membership to help covering our costs

Benefits

  • Contribute to a meaningful cause
  • Regular updates about our activities (WhatsApp group)

Duties

  • Minimum membership fee of 100 CHF (50 CHF for students, elderly/disabled, unemployed)
